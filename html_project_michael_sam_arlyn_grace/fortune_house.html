About Us

Our Company

House Seafood Restaurant opened for business in November 1990 and has been delighting diners ever since. Significant renovations of the restaurant in December 2008 have elevated the dining experience for customers by improving comfort and ambiance.

The restaurant is located in Metropolis at Metrotown - the largest and most vibrant shopping centre in the city of Burnaby. Convenient Skytrain and bus services are close by and the restaurant can be accessed from within the mall or through the Central Boulevard entrance beside the Skytrain station.
