Public Transit:

  • Bus
  • SkyTrain
  • Sea Bus
  • WestCoast Express
Bus
Our vast network of buses, trolleys and community shuttles link to everywhere throughout Metro Vancouver.
SkyTrain
SkyTrain is the oldest and one of the longest automated driverless light rapid transit systems in the world.
SeaBus
SeaBus is a passenger only ferry that crosses the Burrard Inlet, connecting downtown Vancouver with the North Shore.
Westcoast Express
West Coast Express is a commuter rail service operating during peak morning and evening periods between downtown and Mission.
